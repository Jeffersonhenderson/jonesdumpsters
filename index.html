Frequently Asked Questions

Will I get hit with hidden fees when renting a dumpster?

No. What you see is what you pay. We give you upfront pricing from the start. The only time an extra charge comes up is if the dumpster gets overfilled because then it takes extra work to haul it.

How fast can I get a dumpster delivered?

Pretty fast. If you call us in the morning, there’s a good chance we can deliver it the same day. If not, next-day service is always on the table. That way, you’re not stuck waiting while the trash piles up.

What kind of waste can I put in a roll-off dumpster?

Roll-offs are strongly built for heavy or bulky waste like construction debris, roofing shingles, old furniture, yard waste, and even demolition waste. Their swing doors by the side save the stress of wrestling heavy junk over the top.

Can I pick when the dumpster is dropped off and picked up?

Absolutely. We work around your clock, not ours. Whether you want it gone in a few days or sitting there for a month while you take your time, you set the schedule.
